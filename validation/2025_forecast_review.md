## Why This Document Exists

In October 2025, this framework produced a set of forward-looking probability estimates and watch windows based on historical pattern timing. Those estimates are now fully observable through May 2026.

A researcher willing to mark predictions wrong gains standing that decades of correct predictions cannot purchase.

This document records every forecast, the actual outcome, and an honest assessment. Nothing has been adjusted retroactively.

---

## On the Distinction Between Prediction and Pattern-Watching

The October 2025 documents used language that was sometimes more precise than the method warrants. The -30 to -40% figure cited for 2025 was the historical average decline for comparable periods — a reference point drawn from the dataset, not a committed forecast of a specific outcome.

The framework is correctly described as pattern-watching: identifying historical conditions and timing windows worth monitoring, not claiming specific events will occur. That distinction matters for reading this validation honestly.

**Assessment: PARTIALLY CONFIRMED**

The 2025 event is classified as a correction, not a full bear market. Pattern-watching framing: the watch window identified the right moment and mechanism; the historical reference for depth did not predict actual depth.

## Key Observations From This Validation

**What the framework got right:**
- 2025 correction occurred in the predicted timeframe (year 1, Q1-Q2)
- Tariff trigger identified correctly
- Consumer delinquency as a leading indicator — threshold breached, though earlier than forecast
- Gold as a leading indicator — directionally correct, magnitude exceeded expectation
- Partisan pattern (Republican second term, crash/correction in year 1) continued

**What the framework got wrong or missed:**
- Depth of 2025 correction: -19% actual vs. -30 to -40% historical reference
- Speed of 2025 recovery: +17% full year was faster than comparable historical recoveries
- Consumer delinquency timing: fired 12–18 months before the forecast window
- Gold magnitude: $5,600 peak was not anticipated at that level

**What this means for the framework:**
The pattern-watching approach correctly identified the when and the why of 2025 stress. The historical reference points for depth and duration did not accurately describe the actual outcome. The framework is useful for identifying windows and mechanisms; it is not a depth or duration predictor.
